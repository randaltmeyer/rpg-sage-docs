Dice

The second of the two primary uses of RPG Sage is its dice feature. At its most basic, it enables you to roll dice and post the results.

The simplest form of this is the humble [1d20] roll.

As you get more comfortable using it, you will find that you can enter multiple rolls (each with their own description) into a single dice command so that RPG Sage can group the resulst together to make it easier for your group to find and read dice rolls that belong together. The most common form of this is the attack and damage roll combo of d20 games: [1d20 attack; 1d8 damage].

You can, of course, include modifiers to die rolls (even more dice types) and even give each modifier a description should you choose to. For example, a common d20 rogue might roll [1d20 + 8 dagger attack; 1d4 piercing + 2 strength + 1d6 sneak].


While this example shows the user only posting a dice rolls, RPG Sage will read entire messages and respond to all instances of dice rolls that it finds within each message.
